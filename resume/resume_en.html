□ Career Summary

 After gaining three years of experience in product development at a lady's shoe company I joined as soon as I graduate from a university, I have repeatedly changed careers to other industries, such as advertising and IT, in order to expand my design field. The knowledge of product design that I have accumulated professionally since my university days, plus the practical experience in graphic and IT companies, has allowed me to upgrade my knowledge and skills that other product designers do not have. I'm trying to embody a new way of design that can respond to the social needs of a society that is now switching from "tangible consumption to intangible consumption".
